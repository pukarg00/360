HISTORY OF WATCH

The watch was developed by inventors and engineers from the 16th century to mid 20th century as a mechanical device powered by winding a mainspring which turned gears and then moved the hands;it kept time with a rotating balance wheel. In the 1960s the invention of quartz watch which ran on electricity and kept time with a vibrating quartz crystal,proved a radical rise in watchmaking indutry. During the 1980s quartz watches took over the market from mechanical watches,a process referred to "quartz crisis"

Impact of Technology on watch Industry

The watch Industry has been around for centuries, and overtime it has evolved to meet the constant change in need and preferences of the consumer.As Technology advance,the watch industry is also changing.Due to Technological advancement there is rise of smartwatches, automation in manufacturing.

SMARTWATCHES:a major cause of revolution of the watch market

The rise of SMARTWATCHES,led by brands like Apple,Samsung etc has redefined consumer expectations.Today watches are not only used for time checking,but smartwatches offer various function like health tracking,GPS,notification, apps etc

Preferences of consumer in Watches

We can see dastrict shift in preference from traditional mechanical watches to multifunctional smartwatches.People also select watches according to brand value like ROLEX.Many consumer now prioritize functionality ,choosing smartwatchesor fitness trackers with various features like health monitoring,GPS etc.
